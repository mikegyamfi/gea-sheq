ISO Courses – Internal and lead Auditor Courses in ISO 14001

The ISO 14001 is an Environmental Management System (EMS) which provides a system for measuring and improving an organisation's environmental impact. This standard aims to reduce an organisation's waste, pollution and energy consumption.

ISO Courses – Internal and lead Auditor Courses in ISO 45001

The ISO 45001 is an Occupational Health and Safety Management System (OHSMS) which provides a system for measuring and improving an organisation's health and safety impact. This standard focuses on an organisation's internal environment.

ISO Courses – Internal and lead Auditor Courses in ISO 9001

The ISO 9000 family of standards is related to quality management systems and designed to help organizations ensure that they meet the needs of customers and other stakeholders while meeting statutory and regulatory requirements related to the product.

The Chartered Institute of Procurement and Supply (CIPS) Certification Course

CIPS Certification Programme is an integrated study programme, which helps prepare participants through an enhanced self-study programme to achieve CIPS qualifications.

Conduction of stage 1,2 and 3 audits in ISO 9001, 14001, and 45001

Stage 1 audit is an internal audit conducted by the firm or the organization. Stage 2 audit involves audit by customers and suppliers. Stage 3 audit is a certification audit in the ISOs.

Preparation of EMP and Policies

An EMP is a site-specific plan developed to ensure that all necessary measures are identified and implemented in order to protect the environment and comply with environmental legislation.

Preparation of SMP and Policies

Safety management policy is a excessive advantage in making both physical and human resources and reducing financial losses and liabilities. By controlling the risks, each organisation is able to reach an improved performance in health and safety issues.

Preparation of SHEQ Management Systems Plan and Integration Management System Plan

A safety management system is an established set of processes to manage health and safety and maintain a high safety standard in the workplace.

Conduction of Risk Assessments for Projects

A risk is a situation that can either have huge benefits or cause serious damage to an organization. Risk assessment is a term used to describe the overall process or method where you: Identify hazards and risk factors that have the potential to cause harm (hazard identification).

Conduction of EIA for new projects

EIA is intended to identify the impacts (both beneficial and adverse) of a proposed public and private development activities. EIA is mainly used at the level of specific developments and projects.

Institution of Occupational Safety and Health(IOSH)

IOSH is the chartered professional body for health and safety in the workplace. It acts as a champion, supporter, adviser, advocate and trainer for those who protect the safety, health and wellbeing of others.

ISO 31000 - Risk Management

This contains guidelines and principles, as well as a framework and a process for managing risks. It can be used by any organization regardless of its size, activity or sector.
