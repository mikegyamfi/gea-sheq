Welcome

GEA SHEQ Africa Consult is made up of highly trained and experience Safety, Health, Environment and Quality professionals who have dedicated themselves for the capacity building of SHEQ professionals in industry and Academia.

The Consult seeks to provide consultancy services in all Areas of SHEQ and helping to address and reduce supply chain risk of businesses.

What are our concerns?

Safety

Anything to do with accidents, procedures or hazards in the workplace environment that can cause harm to your workforce.

Environment

From gas releases to spills, waste and any other items related to your carbon footprint and environmental sustainability.

Health

This is anything that can affect health from exposure such as hazardous diseases.

Quality

Quality management is the focus on all business activities that are needed to provide a consistent product or service.

What we seek to achieve?

We know that the dangers and risk created by the scope of works being completed, the environment in which we work and each individual’s actions and behaviours form the angle of carrying a safe project, and safeguarding the security of our teams and other stakeholders.
At GEA SHEQ Africa Consult we believe that having a safe and environmentally responsible worksite should not be difficult. Setting clear requirements, collecting information and creating valuable insights should be available to everyone.

We are here to help you succeed with your SHEQ.

Services we operate

We have the license from Examplar Global to run:

ISO Courses – Internal and lead Auditor Courses in ISO 9001, 14001, 45001

The ISO 9000 family of standards is related to quality management systems and designed to help organizations ensure that they meet the needs of customers and other stakeholders while meeting statutory and regulatory requirements related to the product.

The Chartered Institute of Procurement and Supply (CIPS) Certification Course

CIPS Certification Programme is an integrated study programme, which helps prepare participants through an enhanced self-study programme to achieve CIPS qualifications.

Conduction of stage 1,2 and 3 audits in ISO 9001, 14001, and 45001

Stage 1 audit is an internal audit conducted by the firm or the organization. Stage 2 audit involves audit by customers and suppliers. Stage 3 audit is a certification audit in the ISOs.

Preparation of SMP and Policies

Safety management policy is a excessive advantage in making both physical and human resources and reducing financial losses and liabilities. By controlling the risks, each organisation is able to reach an improved performance in health and safety issues.
